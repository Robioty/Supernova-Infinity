HOW TO PLAY

👆Drag to aim, release to drop. A ghost shows where it'll land.
💥Matching planets merge — chain them fast for COMBO bonuses!
🤚Tap HOLD to store your current planet and swap back later. One use per drop.
⚠️Don't let planets cross the danger line for too long!
🌀Tap G to flip gravity. Slow recharge — or catch a ☄️ comet for instant refill!
🌬️Solar Wind events push planets sideways for 30s. Aim against it!
🕳️Reach Nexus and it becomes a Black Hole, consuming small planets to clear space.
🌌Spend Stardust in CONSTELLATION for permanent upgrades.
