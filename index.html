HOW TO PLAY

👆Drag to aim, release to drop. A ghost shows where it'll land.
💥Matching planets merge — chain fast for COMBO bonuses!
🤚Tap HOLD to bank your planet. One use per drop.

🌀Tap G to flip gravity — or catch a ☄️ comet for instant refill!
🛡️Your first near-miss each run is automatically saved — top planets are cleared!
🌅Daily Run: every player gets the same planet sequence today. Compare scores!
⚔️RANKED mode earns/loses RP. FREE PLAY is pressure-free.
